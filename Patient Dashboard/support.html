Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to the account settings and clicking on "Change Password."

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, navigate to the "Appointments" section from your dashboard and select the available slots.

What should I do if I encounter an error on the platform?

If you encounter an error, please refresh the page. If the issue persists, contact us using the form below or the provided email address.
